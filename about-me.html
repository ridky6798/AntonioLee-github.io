About Me

Hello! My name is Antonio, I am a marketer.
With a background in marketing management at Ryerson University,
I possess a strong foundation in marketing analytics, digital marketing, e-commerce, and branding.

I am a South Korean who has studied in Canada since 2009. After high school, I embarked on a career in Science at the University of Toronto. However, my path took an unexpected turn when I decided to serve in the military in South Korea for two years.

During my time in the military, I discovered a deep passion for marketing. This realization prompted me to transfer to Ryerson University, where I focused on Marketing to pursue my newfound interest. With a unique background encompassing both scientific studies and military service, I bring a diverse perspective and a strong sense of discipline to my approach.

Now, equipped with a solid foundation in both science and marketing, I am driven to make a meaningful impact in the marketing field. I'm excited to apply my skills, creativity, and dedication to drive success for organizations and projects. Let's collaborate and create exceptional marketing experiences together.
