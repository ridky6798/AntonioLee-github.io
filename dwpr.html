DORIAN WHO Project:
Noor, Press Release

This content was strategically developed to generate awareness and promote the release of
DORIAN WHO's collection, Noor. The primary emphasis was on compelling brand storytelling,
accompanied by showcasing the inspirations behind the project.

KEY RESULTS: The launch of the collection was met with resounding success as the promotional contents were exclusively shared among accredited stakeholders, including journalists, influencers, and esteemed business partners.

To uphold the brand's image, a minimalistic approach was adopted in the content creation. By embracing simplicity and elegance, the promotional materials effectively conveyed the essence of the collection. Notably, the brand logo underwent a strategic transformation, with the color being changed to black to align with the overall collection and enhance visual cohesiveness.

Furthermore, the content delivered the designer's inspiration about the collection, showcasing their creative vision, the brand's identity, and their mission. This added depth and meaning to the promotional materials, resonating with the target audience and fostering a stronger connection to the brand.

The meticulous distribution of the promotional materials, combined with the deliberate design choices and the inclusion of the designer's inspiration, contributed to a successful launch. The positive feedback received further solidified the brand's reputation among key stakeholders in the industry, positioning it as a leader in the fashion market.
